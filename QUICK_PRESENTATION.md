# JobScraper AI - Quick Presentation Guide

## 1. INTRODUCTION (30 sec)
"Hi, I'm [Name]. I built JobScraper AI - an intelligent job tracking platform that automates job searching and uses AI to generate tailored resumes. It solves the problem of spending hours manually searching for jobs and customizing resumes for each application."

---

## 2. THE PROBLEM (30 sec)
"Job searching is broken: 
- Too time-consuming across multiple platforms
- 75% of resumes get rejected by ATS systems before humans see them
- Tailoring resumes manually for each job is tedious

We built JobScraper AI to fix all three problems."

---

## 3. THE SOLUTION (1 min)
"JobScraper AI has three core features:

**Automated Job Scraping**: Scrapes Indeed and LinkedIn without APIs, applies your filters automatically

**AI Resume Optimization**: Uses Google Gemini Pro to analyze job descriptions and generate ATS-optimized resumes with 90+ keyword match rates

**Batch Processing**: Generate multiple tailored resumes at once, download as Word documents"

---

## 4. HOW IT WORKS (1 min)
"Technically:
- **Scraping**: Selenium with Undetected ChromeDriver to bypass anti-bot measures
- **Matching**: scikit-learn TF-IDF for skill matching and job filtering  
- **AI**: Gemini Pro with sophisticated prompt engineering for resume optimization
- **Stack**: Python/Flask backend, MySQL database, Bootstrap frontend"

---

## 5. KEY FEATURES (45 sec)
"Smart filtering based on sponsorship, skills, experience
Batch resume generation - 5 jobs at once
Format flexibility - upload PDF/DOCX, get Word docs back
Job tracking dashboard to manage applications
All in one platform"

---

## 6. CHALLENGES SOLVED (45 sec)
"**Challenge 1**: Scraping without APIs → Built robust Selenium scrapers
**Challenge 2**: ATS optimization → Extensive prompt engineering with Gemini Pro
**Challenge 3**: Performance → Optimized speeds, batch processing
**Challenge 4**: Formatting → Python text cleaning functions"

---

## 7. IMPACT (20 sec)
"Saves hours per application. Instead of 2-3 hours tailoring resumes, users generate optimized resumes in minutes. Higher response rates because resumes pass ATS screening."
